Green Chemistry Principles

Comprehensive overview and practical applications, emphasizing actionable items and real-world implementation strategies, directly contributing to SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption and Production).

  • Design for prevention: minimize hazards and waste at the source.
  • Use safer solvents & auxiliaries; consider solvent-free conditions.
  • Design for energy efficiency (lower T/P, alternative energy inputs).
  • Real-time monitoring to prevent by-products; catalysis over stoichiometric reagents.

Sustainable Reagents & Solvents

Selection and sourcing of greener chemicals, focusing on less hazardous alternatives and renewable feedstocks, supporting SDG 3 (Good Health and Well-being) and SDG 12.

  • Solvent substitution guides (e.g., swap DMF/DMSO to 2-MeTHF/EtOAc/MeOH where feasible).
  • Prefer bio-based/renewable feedstocks and low-toxicity reagents.
  • Maintain an inventory with hazard bands and recycling options.
  • Supplier transparency: SDS, origin, and waste/disposal notes.

Energy & Resource Efficiency

Techniques to minimize energy and resource use, including “low grid-time” scheduling for compute (e.g., off-peak DFT runs), aligning with SDG 7 (Affordable & Clean Energy) and SDG 13 (Climate Action).

  • Lower temperature profiles; microwave/flow options to cut time/energy.
  • Heat-integration and equipment scheduling around off-peak hours.
  • Water-smart workups; vacuum/rotavap best practices to reduce losses.
  • Track kWh per gram product; benchmark and improve.

Waste Management

Best practices for waste reduction, recycling, and disposal, emphasizing circular economy principles and quantifiable targets—supporting SDG 6 (Clean Water and Sanitation) and SDG 12.

  • Set PMI/E-factor targets per project; prioritize solvent recovery.
  • Segregate waste streams (aqueous/organic/halogenated/solid) correctly.
  • Closed-loop reuse for wash solvents and rinses where validated.
  • Document & audit disposal routes; reduce hazardous fractions.

Tools & Calculators

Online calculators for atom economy, E-factor, water/energy use, plus AI-assisted route prediction to minimize waste before experiments—supporting SDG 9 (Industry, Innovation & Infrastructure).

  • Atom Economy, PMI, E-factor, Solvent Intensity dashboards.
  • Sankey/radar charts for trade-offs; sensitivity analysis sliders.
  • Solvent recommender (hazard bands, bp/polarity compatibility).
  • Scenario A/B/C route compare with “greener variant” suggestions.

Case Studies

Real-world examples of sustainable practices in diverse research settings, highlighting successful implementations and lessons learned—demonstrating practical contributions to the UN SDGs.
